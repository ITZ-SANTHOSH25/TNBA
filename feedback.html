❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How often can I donate blood? ▾

You can donate whole blood every 3 months (90 days). Platelet donation can be done every 2 weeks.

Is blood donation safe? ▾

Yes, blood donation is completely safe. Sterile, disposable equipment is used for each donor.

How long does donation take? ▾

The actual donation takes about 8-10 minutes. The entire process including screening takes 30-45 minutes.

What should I do after donating? ▾

Rest for 10-15 minutes, drink plenty of fluids, avoid heavy exercise for 24 hours, and keep the bandage on for 4 hours.
