Who Can Donate Blood? ✅

Most people can donate blood. Check if you meet the basic eligibility criteria below.

✅ Age between 18-65 years
✅ Weight above 50 kg
✅ Hemoglobin above 12.5 g/dL
✅ Normal blood pressure
✅ No major surgery in last 6 months
✅ Not pregnant or breastfeeding
✅ No infectious diseases
✅ Last donation was 3+ months ago

Health Tips for Donors

Follow these guidelines for a safe donation experience

🍎

Before Donation

  • Get a good night's sleep
  • Eat a healthy meal
  • Drink extra water
  • Avoid fatty foods
  • Bring valid ID
💪

During Donation

  • Stay relaxed and calm
  • Squeeze the stress ball
  • Inform staff if uncomfortable
  • Don't look at the needle
  • Keep breathing normally
☕

After Donation

  • Rest for 10-15 minutes
  • Have juice and snacks
  • Drink plenty of fluids
  • Avoid heavy exercise
  • Keep bandage on for 4 hrs
